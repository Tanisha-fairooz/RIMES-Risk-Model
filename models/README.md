

### **Agricultural Risk Modeling Workflow**

*   **Phase 1: Exploratory Analysis**
    *   Conducted **Ordinary Least Squares (OLS) regression** to analyze the relationships between climate variables (ENSO, IOD, rainfall) and crop production.
*   **Phase 2: Predictive Modeling**
    *   Applied **Random Forest models** for both regression and classification tasks.
    *   Utilized **time-series cross-validation** to ensure model robustness and performance.
*   **Phase 3: Probabilistic Risk Assessment**
    *   Developed a **Bayesian Logistic Regression model** using the **PyMC** library.
    *   Estimated probabilistic **crop loss risk** while providing comprehensive **uncertainty quantification**.
